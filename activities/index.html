Attractions and Acitivities

The central location means that you are ideally located to take advantage of all that Prince Edward Island has to offer for outdoor activity, entertainment, shopping, or business activity.

Make sure to spend some time with the Prince Edward Island Visitor's Guide on-line so you don't miss any of the many things to do while visiting PEI.

If your the outdoor type, you'll be close to

  • Our Famous Beaches
  • Our Many Excellent Golf Courses
  • Kayaking Adventures
  • Deep Sea Fishing
  • Cycling
  • Harness Racing

Charlottetown and the nearby resort areas host a variety of excellent theatre and dining experiences for your night life.

  • Confederation Centre of the Arts - hosts world class theatre including our famous Anne of Green Gables
  • Victoria Playhouse - in picturesque Victoria by the Sea
  • Kings Playhouse - Eastern PEI's historic theatre
  • City Cinema for a cozy film experience
  • The Buzz - check this out for schedules and articles on current art, theatre, and music events
  • Dining! - too many options to mention! but check out some of the suggestions here on the PEI Visitor's Guide

You can shop in our many stores or try some of our many boutique and craft venues

  • Arts and Crafts
  • Shopping

    • The University, Atlantic Technology Centre, and the West Royalty Industrial Park are among a few of the business opportunities located centrally in the Charlottetown area.
