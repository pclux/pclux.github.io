Your Home Away From Home...on Prince Edward Island


Welcome to the Suite at 251 North River Road. This uptown executive-style suite is simply and elegantly furnished, with a completely equipped designer kitchen and full bath as well as an in-suite laundry room. With 1200 square feet and a separate entrance you are assured of all the privacy and comforts of home.


Located "uptown" on a tree-lined street in the heart of Charlottetown, the suite is conveniently close to shopping, theatres, the University and business sectors. Our famous north shore beaches are a scenic 25 minute drive away.



This suite is perfect for the business traveler, honeymooners, and retirees who are looking for quiet space, privacy, comfort, and a central location while visiting PEI. Be sure to visit the interior and grounds of the suite on our Images page.


This fully serviced Suite includes:

  • Private Telephone/Fax/Printer
  • Cable Television
  • Wireless High Speed Internet Access
  • Air Conditioning
  • Private Parking
  • Roof Top Deck with Barbecue
  • In Suite Full Laundry Facilities
  • Weekly Maid Service
