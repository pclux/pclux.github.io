Location

251 North River Road Suite is located on Route 236 and is central to everything on Prince Edward Island and well located for activities in the Provincial Capital of Charlottetown. Prince Edward Island is in the Atlantic Time Zone or GMT -4 hours.

Getting Here

  • Several airlines arrive and depart from the Charlottetown Airport
    • After leaving Charlottetown Airport turn left onto Brackley Point Road. At the second traffic light turn right onto Belvedere Avenue. At the fourth traffic light turn left onto North River Road. The Suite is on the right at 251 North River Road, just before the first traffic light at Nassau Street.
  • You can drive to the Island by Car Ferry
    • Enter Charlottetown via Grafton Street. After the bridge (Hillsborough Bridge) turn right at the fourth traffic light, onto University Avenue. Proceed to the fourth traffic light and turn left onto Nassau Street. At the second traffic light, turn right onto North River Road. The Suite is at the first driveway on the left (251 North River Road).
  • or on the 12.9 kilometre Confederation Bridge.
    • Follow the TransCanada Highway towards Charlottetown. Turn right onto the first street (North River Road) after the Holiday Inn Express. The Suite is at 251 North River Road (just before the 4th traffic light on the right at Nassau Street).
